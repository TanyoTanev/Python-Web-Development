Established in 2021, PV SOL Holding is one of the leading players in the South-East Europe PV forecast-as-a-service sector. Out employees have been awarded with various scientific awards including the know-how of the company. The companies that entrusted us their forecasts are with combined more than 30 MW installed capacity. We have gained significant experience in engineering, installation, operation and maintenance of PV systems (on-grid, off-grid and hybrid). Vision – Innovative system integrator on the decentralized market of production, storage and consumption of energy. Mission – Affordable energy for all by smart innovative systems and cost-effective solutions using the power of the sun. Business model – High environment and quality standards, cooperation with our clients and care for their investments.
